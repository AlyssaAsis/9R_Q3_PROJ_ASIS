What to do in case of fire?

Rescue anyone in the immediate danger area if you can safely do so.

Rescuing a fellow citizen would also have your life at risk. Going through fire would not be easy as our bodyâ€™s are not naturally adapted on very high temperature levels. If an individual is in the burning building, letting the fire fighters do their job would be the best option as they are trained and equipped in these kinds of situations.

Evacuate to a safe area

As humans, it is normal for us to conclude in panicking whenever we are aware of danger. This gives us a difficult state of mind to lead us into safety. Giving us the importance of an evacuation plan. Evacuation plans are usually seen pasted everywhere in buildings. Giving them a map to a route to safety.

In planning evacuation plans, we must know our surroundings. Knowing all possible exits, and danger areas for fire. Having evacuation plans not only gives us safety, but also an ease of mind to when in danger.

Call for help

After safely evacuating a burning building, call the nearest fire station! After calling the fire station, asking for help on neighbors could also be useful. As they can be extra manpower in stopping the fire. Always remember, a plan would always save a life!
